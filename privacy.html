uninstrusive social sharing

shareNice provides website owners with a social sharing widget that does NOT track their users. shareNice is an open-source sharing library that enables your users to share your content with their friends without being tracking by another party.

Privacy Policy

Empowering users by respecting their privacy is what shareNice is all about. shareNice will NEVER:

  • drop ANY cookies on your users.
  • generate logs files on our servers.
  • collect ANY information about its or your users.
  • ... as a result we have NO data to share with ANYONE.

More information about how we maintain your users' privacy can be found on our technical information page.

Finally is it worth mentioning that shareNice is NOT a money making venture and we have NO intention of monetizing the way this service operates.
